## 📋 How to Use This Guide

This guide is structured as a **complete reference manual** covering every aspect of Manual Testing. Each part is a standalone document that can be studied independently, but they are ordered for progressive learning.

**For interview preparation**: Focus on the "Common Interview Questions" section at the end of each part.  
**For daily reference**: Use Parts 6, 7, and 8 as quick-reference guides for test case writing, defect management, and JIRA.  
**For career growth**: Start with Part 12 to understand modern trends and where the industry is heading.
